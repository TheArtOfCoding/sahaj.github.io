What is Sahaj Samadhi Meditation?

In Sanskrit, Sahaj means effortless. Samadhi is Deep meditation - a natural state beyond waking, sleeping and dreaming that is an unlimited reservoir of energy, intelligence and creative power and a place of infinite peace and tranquility.
